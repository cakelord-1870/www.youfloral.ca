Category: Bulk Greenery
Greenery Details

Overview

  • This variety has a more muted smell than the other eucalyptus, has a long vase life, its leaves are short oblong ending in a point.
  • It is great in bouquets because it has a sturdy stem, helping create structure for other more floppy greens.
  • Occasionally this variety is unavailable, if so well substitute with gunni eucalyptus.

Number of Stems

  • Growers bunch – bunches weigh approximately 200 grams
Greenery Care

For information about general greenery care and important notes about quality, check out our Care Guide

Quality Help

Recipes are made to be tweaked, but here is a general rule of thumb for quantities of eucalyptus:

  • Bridal Bouquet: 1/3 bunch
  • Bridesmaid Bouquet: 1/4 bunch
  • Boutineers: Sprigs
  • Corsages: Sprigs
  • Centerpieces: 1/4 bunch
  • Large Arrangements: 1-2 bunches
